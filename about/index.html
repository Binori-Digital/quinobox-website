About Quinobox

Crafting premium packaging solutions since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Quinobox emerged from a simple vision: to provide Malaysian businesses with access to premium, custom packaging solutions without the premium price tag.

What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted partner for hundreds of businesses across Malaysia. We've perfected our craft through years of experience, continuous innovation, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most respected packaging providers in Malaysia, serving e-commerce businesses, retailers, SMEs, and enterprises.
